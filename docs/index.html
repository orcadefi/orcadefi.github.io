Welcome to Orca

Like you, we have been seeing the massive rise of decentralized finance. Decentralized finance is powered by smart contracts and has the promise to revolutionize the way decentralized networks operate.

Platform#

The first task we saw we could potentially add value to was the ability to easily access real-time data from different decentralized finance markets. We believe by developing great real-time products for decentralized finance we can push the revolution of decentralized finance a little further. In time we hope to develop an entire platform around real-time DeFi.

Principles#

OrcaDeFi was created with the following principles in mind:

  • Non-custodial
    • We don’t hold your funds
  • Open source
    • We develop tools and try to publish most in open source on our Github. In some scenarios to keep the community safe we do not publish all the platform code.
  • Community powered
    • Thousands of minds are better than a handful. Our innovation is driven by votes granted to the community.

Advice#

Please note that all these products are in development and they are in beta. Use at your own risk! We make no guarantees on performance or any kind of promises regarding OrcaDeFi.
